I plan editorial calendars, produce assets, and bake content workflows into the product so teams move faster with fewer bottlenecks.

Case Study · FAccounts BOT

Automating order flows and payments via Telegram to cut repetitive manual work.

Apr 2025 – Present

Problem

Manual order taking across chat channels, inconsistent data entry, and error-prone payment confirmation increased turnaround time and support load.

Solution

  • Telegram bot with guided flows (validate input, enforce schema).
  • Automation for payment checks and order status updates.
  • Admin dashboard snippets (quick actions, audit log).

Result

  • −32% handling time for standard orders (pilot).
  • −60% errors from manual entry (validation + required fields).
  • Better visibility: unified log of conversations & status.

Stack
